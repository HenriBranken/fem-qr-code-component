### What I learned

Using variables in `.css` can avoid a great deal of repetition.

```css
:root {
  /* . . . . Color Scheme . . . . */
  --col-bg: hsl(212, 45%, 89%);
  --col-text: hsl(216, 15%, 48%);
  --col-heading: hsl(218, 44%, 22%);
  --col-surface: hsl(0, 0%, 100%);
}
```
